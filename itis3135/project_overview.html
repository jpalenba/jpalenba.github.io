Project Overview

Cut & play

The Cut & Play Salon website will offer a smooth and engaging experience for parents looking to book haircuts for their kids. The site will feature easy- to-use buttons that allow users to schedule, reschedule, or cancel appointments at their convenience. A messaging option will be available to directly contact a hairdresser for any specific questions or requests. To help parents prepare their little ones for a stress-free visit, the site will include helpful tips and service details. Visitors can also explore a fun gallery showcasing before-and-after photos of happy clients, along with brief introductions to each hairdresser, helping kids feel more comfortable and familiar before their appointment.

Dynamic Functionality

Multiple sections throughout the site will employ dynamic forms that streamline tasks like scheduling, rescheduling, and canceling appointments. For instance, dropdown menus may be configured to display only the appointments relevant to a particular client when posting session notes. A dedicated calendar interface will allow both parents and hairdressers to conveniently view their bookings at a glance; selecting a highlighted date can instantly display details such as the service type, appointment time, and assigned hairdresser. Certain actions, like finalizing invoices or updating appointment information, will also trigger automated emails containing client or booking details pulled directly from the site’s records. Powering all of this is a central database that continuously refreshes itself to keep session data, client profiles, and messages accurate in real time. Finally, the About section could feature interactive images or a short slideshow, inviting visitors to explore the salon’s upbeat, child-friendly atmosphere in a more immersive way.
