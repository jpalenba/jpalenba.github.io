About Us – Cut & Play Salon

At Cut & Play Salon, we believe a child's haircut should be more than just a trim — it should be a fun, friendly, and comforting experience for both kids and their parents.

Our site is built to provide a smooth, stress-free experience for families booking a haircut. Whether you’re new or returning, our aim is to make everything — from scheduling to styling — as enjoyable as possible.

What We Offer:

  • Simple Appointment Booking: Schedule, reschedule, or cancel with clearly labeled buttons.
  • Direct Messaging: Send questions or style requests straight to a friendly hairdresser.
  • Helpful Tips for Parents: Advice to prep your child for a happy haircut.
  • Photo Gallery & Introductions: Browse before-and-after shots and meet the stylists.

Our Mission:

We’re here to make haircut day something to look forward to. By combining playful design, skilled professionals, and a warm atmosphere, we create a space where kids feel safe and parents feel supported.
